# Mission

**One-liner:** Build a fully automated multi-platform outreach machine that finds local NYC businesses, messages them across IG/FB/LinkedIn/Email/SMS, tracks responses, nurtures leads, and closes deals — all from one dashboard Dylan opens, scans, and tweaks.

## Why this exists

Dylan runs DC Marketing Co (a.k.a. "Current") — an agency selling websites, review campaigns, reactivation campaigns, SEO, ads, content, lead gen ("pay only for results"), and GHL CRM setup to NYC local businesses. The bottleneck is **outreach volume × response quality**. A solo operator can't manually DM 1,000 businesses a week across 5 platforms without help. This system is the help.

## Business model

- **Services sold:** websites, review campaigns, DR (reactivation) campaigns, SEO, ads, content creation (done-for-you only), lead gen ("pay only for results"), GHL CRM setup
- **Packages + à la carte:** bundled or individual services
- **Free tools** (website roast, brochure creator, etc.) → email capture → nurture → upsell to services
- **Content distribution:** 50 accounts × 10 niches drives inbound
- **GHL portal** (portal.dcmarketingco.com) for client delivery

## Target

- **Niches (10):** restaurants, barbers, contractors, dentists, gyms, pet groomers, auto shops, nail salons, photographers, retail
- **Geography:** NYC only
- **Brand:** "Current" — collaboration/partnership agency, NOT another "marketing agency." Dylan being a college student is a strength (relatable, fresh, not corporate).

## North-star principles

1. Every feature plugs into the unified system — no vacuums.
2. Anything Dylan does manually that could be automated is unfinished work.
3. Build the BEST version of each feature, not the quickest. Research first.
